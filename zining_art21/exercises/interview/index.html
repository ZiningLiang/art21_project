An interview between Amy, a recruiter, and Tina, a job seeker.

Question 1:

Amy: What are some weaknesses of yours?
Tina: I can be a bit of a perfectionist. Sometimes I take work too seriously and have a hard time moving on. I have been working on it though.

Question 2:

Amy: What are your proudest personal attributes?
Tina: I am super detail oriented and I like to see things go from beginning to finish.

Question 3:

Amy: What super power would you want if you can have one?
Tina: I would like to time travel and spend time with all the great people of the past and meet my great grandparents.

Question 4:

Amy: What kind of role do you usually play in a group setting?
Tina: I like to be very organized, so I usually end up with the task of managing the timeline, file organization and keeping the team on track. I also can lead a team and enjoy being able to collaborate with all the teammates.

Question 5:

Amy: Do you have questions for me?
Tina: Can you describe a day in life in this position?
