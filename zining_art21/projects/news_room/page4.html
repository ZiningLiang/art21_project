This little avo prop journey started in September, 2018.

My housemate at the time, Vanessa, and I named it Ferdinand ever since the first photo was taken. Eventually, Ferdinand was left in Vanessa's care after we parted ways by the end of that year.

Two years later, I caught up with Vanessa and was told that Ferdinand had died three months ago. It is a sad news but I was glad to be able to catch up with Vanessa again
