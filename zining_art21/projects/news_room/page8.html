Hi, this is page 8 of the journal.

Right after thanksgiving, I heard from mom that the gorgeous hanging basket of succulents we bought together back in the summer had died in the snow. She forgot to bring it back inside.

I was very sad about it but fortunately, I prolifted from that basket! I have duplicates of the same plants that I could pop back into the same basket and revive it during this winter break! I am very glad about this and I might proplift all the plants I like just in case they might die from an accident like this.

The basket might look very bare now but it is put under a grow light system and might spring back in a few months.
